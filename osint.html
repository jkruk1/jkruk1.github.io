Vehicles

Vesssels

MarineTraffic || https://www.marinetraffic.com

Real-time ship trafficking information. Appears to be have somewhat more comprehensive coverage.

VesselFinder || https://www.vesselfinder.com/

Similar service.

FleetMon || https://www.fleetmon.com/

Fleet trafficking website, with the ability to create daily reports for a "fleet" of vessels of interest. Also has shipspotting photograph database.

Global Fishing Watch || https://globalfishingwatch.org/map

Maritime tracking map with an emphasis on illegal, unreported and unregulated (IUU) fishing. Still have to figure out exactly how it works, but it neetly displays EEZ lines, and is probably going to be of interest to South China Sea watchers.

SAILBOATDATA.COM || https://sailboatdata.com/

Database of sailboats. Doesn't appear particularly comprehensive, but it might be useful one day. Use the "refine search" feature to try to work backwards.

BOAT International || https://www.boatinternational.com/yachts-for-sale

Directory for luxury yachts.

Equasis || http://www.equasis.org

A registry of shipping information developed by the French Ministry for Transport. Information on registration and ownership. Requires hassle-free registration. (Warning: Registration email will send your password in plaintext.)

Aviation

Flightradar24 || https://www.flightradar24.com/

Live air traffic.

ADS-B Exchange Global Radar View || https://global.adsbexchange.com/VirtualRadar/desktop.html

Another live traffic map. Has an API for accessing the data, though I have yet to experiment with it.

ADSBHub || http://www.adsbhub.org/coverage.php

Antoher ADS-B map. Does not appear to have the same search capabilities, but also has ability to get ADS-B data.

FlightAware || https://flightaware.com/

Another all-purpose tracking program. Gives access to 14 days historical data to free users.

OpenSky Network || https://opensky-network.org/

Yet another tracking program. Appears to have a good UI for research.

PlaneFinder || https://planefinder.net/

Ditto. Interface appears pretty tidy, though.

JetPhotos || https://www.jetphotos.com/

Database of aviation photographs.

FAA Aircraft Registry || https://registry.faa.gov/aircraftinquiry/NNum_inquiry.aspx

Registration and ownership information on any aircraft with an N-number.

Trains

RailPictures.net || https://www.railpictures.net/

Photographic database of trains worldwide, over half a million in total.

Railpictures.ca || http://www.railpictures.ca/

Photographic database of trains in Canada.

Archival Resources

Wayback Machine Downloader

https://github.com/hartator/wayback-machine-downloader/blob/master/README.md

An extremely useful tool which allows you to download all pages of a website from the Wayback Machine. After the gem has been installed, navigate to Command Prompt and execute:

wayback_machine_downloader http://example12345.com
