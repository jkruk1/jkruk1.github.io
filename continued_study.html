Khan Academy

https://www.khanacademy.org/profile/jkruk1/

A website with a massive collection of online courses covering all manner of mathematics, science, and the humanities. Good mixture of video lectures and other exercises.

Duolingo

https://www.duolingo.com/profile/JamesKruk

Popular app for learning languages. Very clean UI, with aggressive gamifications and reminders to keep you engaged. Sub-optimal for actually learning the rules of grammar, however.

Udemy

https://www.udemy.com/user/james-kruk/

Online courses, overwhelmingly self-paced lecture format. Provides a certificate upon completion, which is nice, though the quality of the courses varies massively. Prices also fluctuate wildly, and if you wait a few weeks there is almost certain to be a sale of some sort.

Cengage Learning

https://education.gale.com/l-tplmain/SearchResults.aspx

Online classes available through the Toronto Public Library. Varying quality.
